Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES)

MIT School of Engineering, MIT-ADTU Pune

Who are we?

ACES is an autonomous student body, working under the aegis of the Computer Sciuence and Engg. (CSE) department of MIT ADT University. ACES was founded at the start of A.Y. 2018-19 by a few resourseful faculty members who enlisted the help of numerous students for this cause. The cause? To enable students of CSE deptt. to completely harness whatever that is taught as a part of a 4 year B.E course. This will help them to bridge the gap in terms of what is taught in college versus what is required to excel in the industry and life in general.
