ACES is a students association for Dept. of Computer Science & Engg in MIT School of Engineering. This is a platform for the students, by the student & of the students which will enable every student to showcase their leadership skills, technical skills, communication skills. Students without any prior experience will also learn new skills & as well as develop their personalities.

  • Under this platform we regularly perform various workshops, events & hands-on sessions for the students every semester
  • National & International Conferences are held which will give you exposure towards current trends in the industry.
  • Every semester a newsletter will be circulated to all the members of A.C.E.S. informing about various activities (placement/technical/cultural) organized in our college.
  • Teams for competitive programming events will be formed.
